+

2 Year In Australia/UK

At Mid-Valley International College, we offer the Credit Transfer Pathway Program, providing an exceptional opportunity for students to transfer credits to renowned Australian and UK Universities. Students must fulfill the minimum academic and English requirements specified by the respective universities to be eligible for these opportunities. Rest assured, we are constantly expanding our pathway program and anticipate adding more prestigious universities in the near future.
